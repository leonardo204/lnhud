# Privacy Policy — LnHud

**Last updated: April 16, 2026**

## Overview

LnHud is a macOS utility that displays a heads-up display (HUD) when you switch keyboard input sources. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

LnHud does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — The app does not include any analytics frameworks or tracking code.
- **No network access** — The app does not connect to the internet. It has no network entitlements.
- **No personal information** — The app does not access contacts, calendars, location, or any other personal information.
- **No keyboard input logging** — The app only detects *which* input source is active (e.g., "English", "Korean"). It does not read, record, or transmit any keystrokes or text input.

## Data Storage

All user preferences (HUD appearance settings, display options) are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

LnHud does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

LnHud does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this repository.
